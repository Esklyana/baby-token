Baby Token

The Baby Token project aims to use blockchain and AI technologies in telemedicine, employment and financial services for expectant mothers and families through their own mobile applications and products of our partners.

Blockchain-based healthcare, shopping and employment for expectant mothers and families

What can we offer society?

During pregnancy and after childbirth families significantly increase their spending on pregnancy care goods, baby wear and healthcare services. Additional sources of income, especially, from flexible, part-time, and online jobs will be very welcomed by many parents-to-be.

How can we deliver that?

At the moment, mobile applications are the closest to users means of interaction with modern technologies and contribute to the most rapid and efficient adoption. Baby Token team has own mobile applications and technologies to develop such an ecosystem for future parents and families.

We already have top ranked app Pregnancy Tracker. In 2020 we will complement it with Baby Tracker and Period Tracker to have whole ecosystem of applications for families.

Pregnancy Tracker is the first TOP-app in the Baby Token ecosystem!

The app helps millions of expectant mothers track their pregnancy week by week, providing important information, useful exercises and services. The app is available in 6 languages: English, Chinese, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian, French and has a truly global user base spread over 100 countries.

Our business model is based on advertising and affiliate marketing. We have contracts with large FMCG, pharma companies, and online retailers.

What technologies do we use and how?

Blockchain
  • Exchange loyalty points and in-app currencies for Baby Tokens to get more benefits or for world-famous major cryptocurrencies
  • Use blockchain platforms for international micro-payments
  • Crypto asset-backed financial and savings services, online employment for millions of our users based-on smart-contracts
Artificial intelligence and Big Data
  • Collecting personal and health-related data about millions of people, and using a data-driven approach, constantly test new features and improvements over existing user experience
  • AI-based tests for detecting different body conditions and pregnancy disorders
  • Best shopping choice through machine learning and procurement optimization

Baby Token - utility token and an important part of the financial component of the ecosystem.

Baby Token will be used to convert loyalty bonuses and in-app currencies to blockchain assets, as well as to pay fees in the online employment system and system of exchange data generated by users. FAQ
Project Token Team
What is Baby Token project?

Baby Token project will use cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology to process millions of small international payments in Pregnancy Tracker and other mobile applications. Expectant mothers and young parents will use digital currencies to earn daily income, pay for healthcare and telemedicine services, and get access to innovative financial services such as crypto savings accounts, investment plans and even blockchain-based insurance.

Our business cycle thrives on the highest possible customer satisfaction at all sides of the ecosystem. The more users we have, the more advertisers and partners want to join and use our product. At the same time the more partners are listed on our system with their offers, the more users find what they want to use in offered services, bonuses, discounts and promotions.

Who are the users of Baby Token project?

Mainly they are expectant mothers and young parents with children. One of our perspective is to make crypto economy simply accessible and handy for those people who are not familiar with such services.

How are Baby Token project and Pregnancy Tracker connected?

Baby Token project aims to use blockchain and AI technologies in telemedicine, employment and financial services for expectant mothers and families through own mobile applications. Pregnancy Tracker is the first top ranked app in the Baby Token ecosystem developed by our team and successfully works with more than 5 mln users!

What are key brands already working with Pregnancy Tracker?

Famous brands come to Pregnancy Tracker for insights into what expectant women think, how they feel, what products they buy, and when. Since the app launch in 2016 Pregnancy Tracker successfully completed large advertising campaigns for more than 50 international consumer brands, including Similac, Huggies, Libero, Pigeon, Bayer, Johnson & Johnson, Pepsi, Medela and many others. Pregnancy Tracker app supports all major advertising formats, including videos, banners and native ads, and affiliate marketing programs of AliExpress and Amazon.com.

What about the competition?

Our competitors are popular apps and websites such as: BabyCenter, Babytree, Flo Health, Ovia Health, Glow, Clue, Pregnancy+, Meet you, etc.

However, none of our competitors use blockchain & AI technologies as for now. Pregnancy Tracker will be the first app for expectant mothers that will provide flexible online jobs, paying for completing tasks with the ability to convert payments to cryptocurrencies using Baby Token.

Is Baby Token project fully decentralized?

Yes, if we talk about the token itself. No, if we talk about mobile applications. However some features will use decentralized protocols and blockchain platforms.

What is the BABY token used for?

The BABY token is used for all products and services offered by Baby Token project. The users will need BABY tokens to access paid jobs within Pregnancy Tracker and other mobile applications. Exclusively BABY token will be used to convert loyalty bonuses and in-app currencies to blockchain assets, as well as to pay fees in the online employment system and system of exchange data generated by users. In addition to its utility function, BABY tokens will be used inside the app for payments, purchases from the marketplace, telemedicine services, savings and investments.

Will there be an IEO of BABY tokens?

Yes! The IEO is scheduled to Q1 2020 with the listing on a top cryptocurrency exchange (under NDA). Follow our social channels and subscribe to have updates!

Is Baby Token (BABY) an ERC20 token?

Yes. We will place a link to smart contract on Etherscan.

What is the total supply of BABY tokens, and is it increasing?

It’s limited at 2.1 billion tokens. Tokens that were allocated for the sale but weren't sold will be burned.

Who will want to buy and hold BABY token?

BABY will be in circulation inside the Baby Token ecosystem among the users, applications and 3rd party service providers (exchanges, payment solutions, etc.) We are pretty sure that users may become holders of significant amount of BABY tokens as the cryptocurrency of limited supply will demonstrate increasing value and will appreciate as the number of users and 3rd parties, use-cases and range of transaction types utilizing BABY grow.

What is BabyCash in Pregnancy Tracker?

BabyCash is the internal currency of the Pregnancy tracker application. Users can get different rewards as BabyCash and spent on various goods and services. Also BabyCash can be converted to cryptocurrency - Baby Token and so users can enter crypto world. The price of BabyCash is constant (stable). Every registered user in the Pregnancy tracker application has his own BabyCash wallet.
